DO-A-THON

Do-a-thons are events that invite participants for a day of making, collaborating, trying, teaching, designing, testing, and learning in the name of Open Research and Education. Do-a-thons are events focused on gettings things done through open practices, inclusive participation, and careful problem exploration.

Co-designed by OpenCon with a global community that's hosted over 10,000 people at 125+ events, virtual and physical, in more than 50 countries and 30 languages.
